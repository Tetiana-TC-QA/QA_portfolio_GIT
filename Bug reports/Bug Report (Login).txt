Bug Report: 
Login Button Unresponsive on Chrome

Summary:
Login button does not respond when clicked 

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open Google Chrome 
2. Go to the login page: https://utest.com/login
3. Enter valid email and password.
4. Click the Login button.

Expected Result:
User is successfully logged in and redirected to the dashboard.

Actual Result:
Nothing happens. The page stays on the login form. No error is shown.

Environment:
Chrome v125.0.0
OS: Windows 10

Additional Info:
The issue does not occur on Firefox or Safari.

Severity: Medium
Priority: High
